We've all been there - the busy week grinds to a halt and all you want to do is relax! You have an idea of going out with your friends or family but you don't know where to go, you might not know which beaches or parks have a beautiful view, and you certainly don't want to hassle preparing meals for an outdoor luxury picnic.

Why not live like royalty for a day?

Celebrating an anniversary or birthday? You got it! How about a bachelorette party, graduation celebration, or corporate event? Of course!
