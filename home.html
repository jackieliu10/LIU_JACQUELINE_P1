THE EXPERIENCE

FOOD & DRINK

We offer a variety of food options and packages to suit your needs. We work closely with our vendors every morning to handpick the foods to ensure you get the best possible quality. Just select which package you'd like and we'll have it prepped and ready!

  • Beverages (wines, ciders, non-alcoholic)
  • Fruit platters
  • Meat and cheese charcuterie boards
  • Tiered dessert trays

DECOR

We have decor options tailored for a wide selection of events - from birthdays and baby showers to romantic anniversary picnics for two. Contact us with any custom decor requests.

  • Pillows and blankets
  • Balloon arch
  • Birthday balloons
  • Flowers and centerpieces

SETUP & CLEANUP

Our team will take care of the entire setup and cleanup process. We provide the tables, table settings, dishware and drinkware, luxurious pillows and blankets, centerpieces, your selected music, your selected food and drink, and any additional decor as requested.
On the day of your scheduled picnic, all you and your guests have to do is show up at the pre-determined location and enjoy!
