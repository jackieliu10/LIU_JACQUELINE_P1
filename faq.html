FAQ'S

What are luxury picnics?

Luxury picnics are the ultimate picnic experience. Our team scouts out the best locations that can provide both a scenic and comfortable experience. We take care of all of the set-up as well - including food that is hand-selected every morning and decor and ambiance to make sure your picnic is always beautiful. Plus, we clean everything up for you! No need for you or your guests to lift a finger.

What locations do you offer?

Currently, we host picnics in Alameda Beach, Berkeley Marina, Joaquin Miller Park, Grey Whale Cove, Crissy Field, Golden Gate Park, Palace of the Fine Arts, Ocean Beach, Alamo Park, or your home!
If you have somewhere else in mind, please contact us.

What is your refund / cancellation policy?

Any cancellation made a week in advance will receive a full refund minus the loss of the $50 security deposit. If weather impacts your picnic, we will reschedule your picnic for a later date.
